Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Issues

  • Q: What should I do if the application is not loading properly?
    A: Ensure you have a stable internet connection. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser's cache and cookies. If the issue persists, contact our support team at maahir.ahmed@education.nsw.gov.au.
  • Q: Why am I seeing a blank page after logging in?
    A: This could happen due to a temporary server issue or an error in fetching your data. Try logging out and logging back in. If the issue continues, please report it to us.

Data Security and Privacy

  • Q: Is my financial data secure on this platform?
    A: Yes, we take data security seriously. Our platform uses industry-standard encryption methods to protect your personal and financial information. However, we recommend using strong passwords and enabling two-factor authentication for added security.

Additional Support

  • Q: How can I get additional help or support?
    A: For further assistance, please email our support team at maahir.ahmed@education.nsw.gov.au. Include details about the issue you're experiencing, and we'll respond as soon as possible to help resolve your problem.
